Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

Trace the shape shown on screen using one continuous finger stroke. Don't lift your finger! Your drawing is scored based on how closely it matches the template.

How does scoring work?

Your drawing is compared to the template shape pixel-by-pixel. Staying on the lines earns points. Drawing outside them costs points. Harder difficulties require more precision.

How do I unlock more shapes?

Earn stars by scoring well on shapes. Medium shapes unlock at 8 stars, Hard shapes unlock at 20 stars. You earn 1-3 stars per shape based on your score.

What is the Daily Challenge?

Every day there's a new shape that's the same for all players. Complete it to submit your score to the Game Center leaderboard. You can only play it once per day.

How does Draw with Friends work?

Pick a prompt and draw your version. Send a challenge link to a friend — they won't see your drawing until they draw their own. Both drawings are revealed side by side!

Why can't my friend see my drawing?

That's by design! Neither person can see the other's drawing until both have drawn. This makes the reveal more fun.

Does the app work offline?

Drawing Challenge and Free Draw work fully offline. Draw with Friends and Daily Challenge require an internet connection.

How do I delete my data?

Uninstall the app to delete all local data. For CloudKit data (shared drawings), go to Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage.
